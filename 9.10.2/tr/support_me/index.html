    Support me¶

    You can support mastercoms using Liberapay or Ko-fi with money, or gift items through trade offers. There’s also a $2.99 supporter subscription on the Discord server.

    You can also gift me using Steam Wallet Funds! Add me as a friend on Steam (leave a comment about gifting!) Then, you can gift me a Steam Digital Card or gift me a wishlisted game.

    If you cannot directly support me, I have a Support-A-Creator code: mastercoms, which you can set in Fortnite, Rocket League, and other games on the Epic Games Store, as well as purchasing games and DLC on the store itself. In connection with Epic Games’ Support-A-Creator Program, I receive a commission from these purchases.

    You can also support my other projects, like playing on my 8v8 Team Deathmatch map for Fortnite. Island code: 7761-6157-7902

    Lastly, you can give me a Steam Award.

    You have my endless gratitude if you support me with money or gift items, and this support encourages me so much!

    If you cannot support me through the above methods, that’s fine too! Your thank yous are always cherished and mean so much to me!
